FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers for dealers and mechanics looking to buy in bulk.

What is the minimum order for wholesale pricing? ▼

Our minimum order starts at ₹5,000. For better rates and discounts, a monthly purchase of ₹25,000+ is ideal. We're flexible for regular customers — just call and we'll work something out.

Do you deliver across Uttar Pradesh? ▼

Yes, we deliver to most districts across UP via courier or transport. Local customers can also pick up directly from our store for faster turnaround.

Are the parts genuine or aftermarket? ▼

We stock both OEM-quality genuine parts and reliable aftermarket parts from trusted brands. We'll always be transparent about what you're getting so you can make the right choice for your customers.

What payment options do you accept? ▼

We accept UPI, bank transfer, and cash. For trusted regular dealers, short-term credit can be arranged. A proper GST invoice is provided on every order.

How do I place an order? ▼

Simply WhatsApp or call us with the part name, vehicle model, and quantity you need. We'll confirm availability and pricing quickly and dispatch as soon as the order is confirmed.
